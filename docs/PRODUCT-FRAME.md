## Role Of This Document

This is not a business plan.

This document prevents the product from being misunderstood as:

- an AI novel generator
- a prompt collection
- an AGI replacement pitch
- a simple validation tool
- a copyright guarantee product
- a generic productivity SaaS

Correct understanding:

> Loreguard records and packages the process by which a human directed, judged, edited, approved, and took responsibility for AI-assisted creative work.

## Core Sentence

> 해줘는 소비, 하라는 제작.

Loreguard is not “AI writes for you.” Loreguard is a creative IDE where the writer directs AI and keeps a record of creative process, rights context, and release readiness.

As AI becomes stronger, the product need is not only more automation. The field needs role boundaries, permissions, verification, approval, and records.

## AGI 기준

The frame is not AGI denial.

The position:

> Whether AGI arrives or not, real work still needs roles, permissions, responsibility, stop conditions, verification criteria, and records.

AGI ability and operational authority are different. A capable AI still needs a job envelope, reporting format, hold conditions, approval line, and audit trail.

Internal phrase:

> 부자 백수 AI에게 직업을.

Public phrasing:

> AI에게 역할과 책임 경계를 부여합니다.

## WABI

WABI does not give AI personhood.

WABI gives AI a job:

- role contract
- capability profile
- task envelope
- action license
- safety envelope
- verification gate
- process record

Short definition:

> WABI는 AI에게 역할을 맡기고, 그 역할 안에서만 일하게 만드는 작업 프레임이다.

Internal definition:

> WABI = AI 직무 배치 프레임.

## What Loreguard Sells

Loreguard sells:

- control
- creative process records
- human contribution evidence
- approval logs
- source/reference records
- rights/IP summaries
- release packages
- team/institution accountability paths

Loreguard must not sell:

- “AI writes everything”
- “AGI solves everything”
- copyright guarantees
- complete plagiarism prevention
- human creativity certification
- legal responsibility replacement

## Loreguard Business Frame

Customer anxiety and product value:

| Customer anxiety | Loreguard value |
|---|---|
| Will AI use make my creativity look absent? | Human decision/edit/approval logs |
| Can I explain the process later? | Creative process confirmation package |
| Do I need to look professional to platforms/publishers? | Release package |
| Can I sell this as IP? | IP Bible / rights summary |
| Can collaborators dispute contribution? | Contribution/approval/change history |

Core sentence:

> 원고를 파일에서 끝내지 않고, 제출 가능한 IP 패키지로 바꾼다.

## BYOK And Local AI

BYOK and local AI are not the main product.

| Function | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Hosted AI | convenience mode |
| BYOK | cost/model control |
| Local AI | privacy/offline/institution response |
| 3-slot Local AI | role-based local model routing/fallback |

Core:

> AI 모델은 사용자가 고른다. Loreguard는 창작 과정을 전문 자산으로 만든다.

## Redeem And Entitlements

Redeem is not the product itself.

Redeem is an entitlement application path:

- trial extension
- certificate/export package credit
- translation/localization work credit
- publisher/group seat
- event or support grant

Current implementation state:

- Stripe checkout exists behind a feature gate.
- `/api/redeem` does not exist.
- Redeem input UI does not exist.
- Do not present redeem as active until entitlement ledger, receipt, cancellation/revocation, and idempotency are implemented.

Business framing:

> 리딤은 공짜 쿠폰이 아니라, 어떤 권한이 누구에게 왜 적용됐는지 남기는 이용권 기록이다.

Use this when explaining the business to another AI:

> 이 사업은 AI가 대신 결과물을 만들어주는 해줘 시장이 아니라, 인간이 AI를 지휘하고 그 과정의 역할·권한·검증·승인·기록을 남기는 하라 시장 제품군이다.

> 첫 제품 Loreguard는 창작 전문 IDE이며, 원고·세계관·캐릭터·번역·창작 과정 확인서·IP 패키징을 묶어 작품을 제출 가능한 Creative Process Record로 만든다.
